Aiprilene Mesh

Monofilament Polypropylene Mesh (Undyed) Non-Absorbable Sterile Surgical Mesh

Product Overview

Aiprilene Mesh is constructed of knitted filaments of extruded polypropylene, identical to our Aiprilene Suture. This nonreactive material retains its strength indefinitely and provides bi-directional elasticity for hernia repair and other facial deficiencies.

Key Indications:

  • Used for hernia repair (both open and laparoscopic) and other procedures that require a reinforcing or bridging material.
  • Lightweight construction with macro-porous design for high visibility and colonization.
