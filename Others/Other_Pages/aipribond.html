Aipribond

Braided & Coated Polyester Non-Absorbable Surgical Suture U.S.P.

Product Overview

Aipribond is a non-absorbable, braided, sterile surgical suture composed of Poly (ethylene terephthalate). It is prepared from fibers of high molecular weight, long-chain polyesters for optimal handling and visibility.

Key Indications:

  • Indicated for general soft tissue approximation and/or ligation, including cardiovascular, ophthalmic and neurological procedures.
  • Its high molecular weight results in an ultra-strong suture, a choice of leading Orthopaedic Surgeons.
