Aipriseal™

Sterile Bonewax

Product Overview

Aipriseal Bone Wax is a mixture of beeswax, paraffin, and isopropyl palmitate, a wax-softening agent. It is opaque and has a waxy odor. It's indicated for use in control of bleeding from bone surfaces during surgical procedures.

It achieves local haemostasis of bone by acting as a mechanical (tamponade) barrier. It does not act biochemically and is non absorb-able. It is available sterile in individual foil envelopes, each containing 2.5 g and packed in an individually sealed over wraps. Surgical wax is sterilized by Gamma radiation.

Important Usage Instructions

  • Packets should be opened just prior to use to minimize the possibility of contamination and excessive drying
  • Should be used immediately after opening
  • The Surgical Wax should not be re-sterilized or subjected to excessive heat

Indications

  • Control of bleeding from bone surfaces during surgical procedures
  • Local haemostasis of bone by acting as a mechanical barrier

Product Characteristics

  • Helps local haemostasis by functioning like a mechanical barrier
  • Biochemically inert
  • Non-absorbable
  • Sterilized by Gamma radiation
  • Available in individual sterile foil envelopes
