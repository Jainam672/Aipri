Indications

Monofilament Polyamide Suture is indicated for use in general soft tissue approximation and/ or ligation, including use in cardiovascular, ophthalmic and neurological procedures. The best material for skin approximation.

  • Being a monofilament with smooth surface never support bacterial growth followed by capillary action
  • Uniform elasticity supports small movements results cosmetic effect
  • Ultra smooth surface results negligible pain

Ultimate sutures for:

  • Skin Closure
  • Hard Tissues approximation
  • Casualty stitching
